For every SaaS business I've grown, I've leveraged Content Marketing. I've done this for 20+ Years. Founder-led Content Marketing is one of the best ways to drive growth for your SaaS business. But let's be honest....

We've all seen those posts on LinkedIn from a Founder and cringed.

"I got married this past weekend, and here's 5 things it taught me about Founder-led Sales"

Here's what you probably didn't know...

All those shitty Founder-brand posts are being written by the same Ghostwriting Agency.

I've used Content Marketing to drive growth for my companies for 20+ years.

I know there is a huge difference between chewy posts that gets likes...

And actual Content Marketing posts that drives revenues.

There are three guiding principles that have helped me continue the Content game:

1) Focus on a Channel where your ICP hangs out and engage with them

2) Only do posts where you Share Your Journey about (Customer Problem, Customer Success, and Customer Lessons Learned)

3) Always offer a Manifesto that turns Engagement -> Leads

This 3-Point Framework is THE most effective way to do Founder-led Content Marketing.
