how to sign $5k - $10k/mo agency clients with YouTube

most agency owners treat youtube like a brand awareness play.

post some videos, chase views, hope something sticks.

that's exactly why it never makes them money.

the agency owners signing $5k-$10k/mo clients do the opposite.

they don't make content for the algorithm.

they make content for the one buyer watching at 11pm who's looking to switch the agency they work with.

or buyer who scaled to the point they now need to hire an agency quickly.

when you think about it this way, few things change:

you stop optimizing for views and start optimizing for the right viewer

every video quietly handles an objection your best clients already have

by the time they book a call, they're pre-sold

this way you don't have to chase or convince

and you don't have to race five other agencies to the bottom on price;)

just clients who found you, trust you, and already want to work with you.
